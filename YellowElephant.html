The Context

After conducting extensive research and gathering feedback from the local community, we found that the majority of residents were frustrated with the limited pharmacy operating hours. This often resulted in individuals having to take time off work or rearrange their schedules to collect their medication. To address this issue, our team developed an easy-to-use application that enables users to order their medication from the comfort of their own homes, and have it delivered directly to their doorstep at any time of day or night. The app also includes a variety of features designed to make the process as seamless and convenient as possible, including medication reminders, automatic refills, and real-time updates on delivery status.
Overall, our project has been a resounding success, with the app receiving overwhelmingly positive feedback from the North Greenwich community. Our team remains committed to delivering a reliable and convenient service to all our users, and we are continually exploring new ways to improve and enhance the user experience.
