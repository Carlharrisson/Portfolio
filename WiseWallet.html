The Context

My research showed that many elderly people are struggling with the complexities of using digital wallets and often felt insecure with the technology. I addressed this issue by developing an intuitive digital wallet app tailored specifically to the needs of elderly users. The app provides clear and simple instructions, making it easy to navigate and understand, while also incorporating state-of-the-art security measures to protect users' personal and financial information. I also included additional features such as transaction history and rewards programs to make the app more appealing and useful. The feedback I received from My target audience was overwhelmingly positive, and I am committed to continuing to improve the app's functionality and user experience.
